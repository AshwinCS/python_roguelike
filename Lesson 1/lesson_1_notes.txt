Goals for our game:


1.) Our game is going to be turn based.
2.) Our game is going to be a dungeon crawler - with 10 levels.
3.) We will have a player character
4.) We will have two different types of enemies with different AI types for each one.
5.) A few items and spells
6.) Tile based graphical system using PyGame.
	5a.) At first these will be limited tiles
	5b.) We will upgrade to animated sprites
7.) Sound effects and music.
